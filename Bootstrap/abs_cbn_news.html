ABS-CBN News

Mission

We exist to serve the Filipino.
We exist to inform, educate, and entertain through creative content for any distribution platform.
We innovate, diversify, and expand into enterprises where our expertise in content creation opens new business and service opportunities.
We promote the best in Filipino talent, and reflect the true sentiment of the Filipino.

Vision

Transforming lives by changing the media landscape is both ABS-CBN's vision and passion. As our audiences demand greater control over how and when they will consume our content, we will ensure our continued relevance by distributing our content on the widest array of platforms that technology will allow. Our audiences will be able to reach us anytime, in any place, and in any medium and device.
While everything around us changes - the technology, the production process, the medium used to access our content - our core ability to create quality content that touch, inspire, and empower our viewers must remain constant. We will continue to build on our core strength of content creation.
